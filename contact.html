FAQ

HOW FAR IN ADVANCE SHOULD I BOOK?

+

For weddings, we recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance. For portraits and studio sessions, 2–4 weeks is usually sufficient. Contact us to check availability.

HOW LONG UNTIL I RECEIVE MY PHOTOS?

+

Portrait and studio sessions are delivered within 5–7 days. Wedding coverage is delivered within 3–4 weeks. All photos arrive via a private online gallery.

DO YOU TRAVEL OUTSIDE KATHMANDU?

+

Yes! We cover all of Nepal and are available for destination shoots across South Asia. Travel and accommodation fees apply for locations outside the Kathmandu Valley.

WHAT IS YOUR CANCELLATION POLICY?

+

A 50% deposit is required to secure your date. Cancellations made more than 30 days in advance receive a 50% refund. Within 30 days, the deposit is non-refundable.
